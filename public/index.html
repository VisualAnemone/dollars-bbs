> ABOUT THE DOLLARS

The Dollars have no face.

We have no color.

We are students, workers, artists, wanderers. We pass each other on the train every day without knowing. We share the same city, the same silence, the same streets.

This BBS is our only meeting place.

There are no rules except one:
do not cause trouble in the name of the Dollars.

Beyond that — talk. Help. Connect. Disappear.

You were invited here.
That means you are already one of us.
